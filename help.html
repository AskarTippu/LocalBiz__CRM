Getting Started

To add a new customer:

  1. Go to the Customers section from the sidebar
  2. Click the "+ Add Customer" button
  3. Fill in the customer details (Name, Email, Phone, Address)
  4. Add any additional information in the Notes section
  5. Click "Add Customer" to save

To view a customer's profile:

  1. Go to Customers section
  2. Find the customer in the list
  3. Click the View (üëÅÔ∏è) button to see their full profile
  4. From the profile, you can view their visit history, billing info, and notes

To find a specific customer:

  1. Go to the Customers page
  2. Use the search bar at the top to search by name, email, or phone
  3. Results will filter automatically as you type
  4. You can also use the Status filter to narrow results

Billing & Invoices

To generate a new invoice:

  1. Navigate to Billing section
  2. Click "+ Generate Invoice" button
  3. Select the customer and enter invoice details
  4. Add items/services with amounts
  5. Set the due date and click "Generate"

To view unpaid invoices:

  1. Go to Billing page
  2. Use the Status filter dropdown
  3. Select "Unpaid" or "Overdue"
  4. The list will show only unpaid/overdue invoices
  5. Click View to see invoice details

To record an invoice payment:

  1. Go to Billing section
  2. Find the invoice in the list
  3. Click the Edit (‚úèÔ∏è) button
  4. Change status from "Unpaid" to "Paid"
  5. Add payment date and method
  6. Click "Update" to save

Reminders & Calendar

To add a new reminder:

  1. Go to Reminders section
  2. Click "+ Add Reminder" button
  3. Enter reminder details (Customer, Date, Time, Description)
  4. Click "Create Reminder"
  5. You'll receive notifications on the scheduled date

To view your calendar:

  1. Go to Reminders section
  2. The calendar displays all reminders for the current month
  3. Dates with events show the reminder details
  4. Use navigation arrows to switch between months
  5. Below the calendar is a list of upcoming reminders

Account & Settings

To change your password:

  1. Go to Settings
  2. Click "Security" tab
  3. Enter your current password
  4. Enter your new password twice
  5. Click "Update Password"

To update your account information:

  1. Go to Settings
  2. Edit your name, email, phone, or company
  3. Adjust your timezone and preferences
  4. Click "Save Changes" at the bottom

To control your notifications:

  1. Go to Settings
  2. Click "Notifications" tab
  3. Toggle notification types on/off as needed
  4. Choose which events trigger email notifications
  5. Click "Save Preferences"
